About CAASS
The mission of the Center for Academic Advisement and Student Success (CAASS) is to assist undergraduate students in establishing, monitoring, and achieving graduation requirements. We provide student-focused, developmental advisement in an environment that recognizes the unique spirit and individuality of each student. We work with academic departments and administrative offices to develop and maintain a coordinated and comprehensive approach toward advisement.

FAQ

  • Is attending orientation mandatory?

    Yes, you will not be able to register for classes in the Fall 2018 semester if you do not attend orientation.

  • What happens if I miss my orientation appointment?

    As a college student you should do everything in your power to not miss appointments that you have made in advance. However, under extenuating circumstances, if you can not make your appointment, you are allowed to make one more orientation appointment.

  • Can I still register for classes if I do not attend orientation?

    No. You must attend orientation in order to register for classes for your first semester.

  • What do I need to bring to orientation?

    Yourself, your EMPL id, and a positive attitude.

  • Can my parents attend orientation with me?

    No. However, keep a look out for our Parent Orientation.

  • What should I do after orientation?

    Check out our post orientation checklist here

  • How do I make an appointment for orientation?

    You can make an appointment here.

  • What will happen during orientation?

    The itinerary for orientation is is listed here

  • Who do I contact if I have questions?

    If you have any questions regarding orientation, please contact First College Year at 718-951-5471 or email them at firstcollegeyear@brooklyn.cuny.edu
    If you have any questions regarding the enrollment process for Brooklyn College, please contact the Admissions office at 718-951-5001 or email them at adminqry@brooklyn.cuny.edu
    If you have any questions regarding the classes that you have enrolled in, please contact the Center for Academic Advisement and Student Success (CAASS) at caass@brooklyn.cuny.edu or call them at 718-951-5471

  • Where can I find out more information regarding financial aid?

    You can contact the financial aid office at 718-951-5051 or email them at finaid@brooklyn.cuny.edu. You can also check out https://fafsa.gov/ for more information on your FAFSA.
